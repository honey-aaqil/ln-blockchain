Real-World Uses of Blockchain

Supply Chain

Blockchain can be used to create a transparent and immutable record of a product's journey from origin to consumer. This ensures authenticity and prevents fraud. It's a game-changer for logistics.

Farm -> Packer -> Shipper -> Retailer

Healthcare

It can secure patient records, giving patients control over who sees their data. Patient privacy is paramount. This is a big deal1.
Other Areas
  1. Voting Systems: Creating tamper-proof voting records.
  2. Intellectual Property: Proving ownership of digital assets.
  3. Real Estate: Streamlining property sales.
Summary
 The idea that blockchain is just for finance is outdated. The notion that it has no practical use is clearly false. The future potential is enormous and industry-agnostic.
