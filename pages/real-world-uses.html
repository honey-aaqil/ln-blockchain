Not Just for Bitcoin: Exploring Real-World Uses of Blockchain

While Bitcoin made blockchain famous, the technology's potential goes far beyond digital currency. Its ability to create trust is its real power.

Use Case: Supply Chain Management

Blockchain can be used to create a transparent and immutable record of a product's journey from origin to consumer. This ensures authenticity and prevents fraud. 

        Farm ->- Packer ->- Shipper ->- Retailer

Use Case: Healthcare

It can secure patient records, giving patients control over who sees their data. Patient privacy is paramount. This is a big deal1.

Other Exciting Areas
  1. Voting Systems: Creating tamper-proof voting records.
  2. Intellectual Property: Proving ownership of digital assets.
  3. Real Estate: Streamlining property sales.
